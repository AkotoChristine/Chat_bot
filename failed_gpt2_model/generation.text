How do I make Jollof Rice(West Africa)  ? ? Answer: place rice, lentils, sugar, tomatoes (or whatever you like) and water in a bowl. allow rice to sit for at least an hour before cooking. add water, onion and garlic to rice. bring to a boil. let simmer for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally. when rice is very soft, add salt and pepper to taste. let it sit for 20 minutes. it will be very hot. heat a large saucepan over medium heat. when hot, add the oil and bring to a boil. remove from heat and add the lentils. cook until soft and tender, about 2 minutes. season with salt and pepper. stir in the tomato paste. add the rice and cook for 20 minutes more, stirring occasionally. heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. when hot, add the water and garlic powder. cook for 5 minutes. add the onions and fry, stirring often, until slightly translucent, about 5 minutes. add the lentils and cook until slightly softened and beginning to brown, about 1 minute. add the chicken broth and cook for 5 minutes more, /n stirring frequently, until the chicken begins to stir. add the rice and cook for 2 more minutes. add the tomatoes and mix until thoroughly combined. add the milk and stir
